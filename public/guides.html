Guides

All the guides we suggest using to get your hands on these

Footlocker

  1. Go to the Footlocker site
  2. In the search tab, search for "eros"
  3. Select your favorite shoe out of the options
  4. Select size
  5. Click "Add to cart"
  6. Click the checkout button
  7. Fill in payment information and finalize payment
  8. Done!

Nike

  1. Go to the Nike site
  2. In the search section, search "Eros"
  3. The search section will expand with a few different options for shoes
  4. Select the shoe option you would like to purchase
  5. Select size
  6. Click "Add to bag"
  7. Proceed to checkout
  8. Fill in payment information and finalize payment
  9. Done!

Kith

  1. Go to the Kith site
  2. Click on the men's section
  3. A drop down menu will open, click on "Explore Mens"
  4. Scroll down until you see the "Eros" section
  5. Select which shoe you are interested in purchasing
  6. Click "Add to bag"
  7. Proceed to checkout
  8. Fill in payment information and finalize payment
  9. Done!
