Guides

All the guides we suggest using to get your hands on these

Footlocker

  1. Go to the Footlocker site
  2. In the search tab, search for "eros"
  3. Select your favorite shoe out of the options
  4. Select size
  5. Click "Add to cart"
  6. Click the checkout button
  7. Fill in payment information and finalize payment
  8. Done!

Nike

  1. Go to the Nike site
  2. In the search section, search "Eros"
  3. The search section will expand with a few different options for shoes
  4. Select the shoe option you would like to purchase
  5. Select size
  6. Click "Add to bag"
  7. Proceed to checkout
  8. Fill in payment information and finalize payment
  9. Done!

Kith

  1. Go to the Kith site
  2. Click on the men's section
  3. A drop down menu will open, click on "Explore Mens"
  4. Scroll down until you see the "Eros" section
  5. Select which shoe you are interested in purchasing
  6. Click "Add to bag"
  7. Proceed to checkout
  8. Fill in payment information and finalize payment
  9. Done!

Adidas

  1. Go to the Adidas site
  2. Click the search icon, it will then open a search menu
  3. Type in the search bar "Eros" then press enter
  4. Select which shoe you would like to purchase
  5. Select your preferred size
  6. Click on add to bag
  7. After adding a pop up will appear, click on view bag
  8. A bag page will appear, click on Checkout and continue to checkout
  9. Fill out your delivery and payment information then finalize the payment
  10. Done!

Notre-Shop

  1. Go to the Notre-Shop site
  2. Click on the 3 lines in the top left hand side of the site
  3. After the sidebar opens, click in the search bar
  4. While in the search bar type in the "Eros" then press enter
  5. A list of shoes will appear, click on the shoe you would like to purchase
  6. Select your desired shoe size
  7. Click on add to cart, after the added to cart pop up appears, click on checkout
  8. Click on proceed to checkout, Fill out required payment & shipping information and complete the order
  9. Done!

Hanon-shop

  1. Go to the Hanon-Shop site
  2. Click the search icon, it will then open a search bar
  3. Type in the search bar "Eros" then press enter
  4. Select which shoe you would like to purchase
  5. Click on Go to Checkout
  6. Proceed to checkout, Then fill out billing and payment information
  7. After filling out the information, finalize the purchase and complete the order
  8. Done!
